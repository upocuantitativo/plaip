Learning Activities

Short exercises, micro-tasks, guided reflections, and digital challenges throughout the learning pathway. These activities are part of the learning process and allow us to observe progression.

Intermediate activities: These do not count as formal assessment. Their purpose is to reinforce learning, practice concepts, and allow the system to collect observable learning signals to adjust the personalised pathway.

Observable Learning Signals

During activities, the platform collects the following signals to adjust the pathway:

  • Correct/incorrect responses
  • Number of attempts
  • Time spent
  • Response revisions
  • Progression between activities
  • Abandonment or persistence
