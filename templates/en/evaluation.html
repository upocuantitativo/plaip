Assessment Types

Checkpoint
Progress Checkpoints

Brief assessments at key learning milestones to verify understanding before moving to more advanced content.

  • 5-10 questions per checkpoint
  • Automatic pathway adjustment
  • Immediate feedback provided
Continuous
Activity Performance

Automatic tracking of performance during regular learning activities. No separate testing required.

  • Real-time signal collection
  • Pattern recognition
  • Trend analysis
