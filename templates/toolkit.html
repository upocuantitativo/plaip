Platform User Guide

Follow these steps to effectively use the InfiniteLearner platform with your students.

1
Initial Setup

Configure your class and register students on the platform.

  • Access the Student Management section
  • Create individual profiles or use bulk import
  • Set demographic and initial skill information
2
Run Initial Diagnostic

Assess students' starting levels across all competences.

  • Access the Diagnostic module
  • Assign diagnostic tests to students
  • Review results to understand baseline levels
3
Assign Learning Activities

Guide students through personalized learning paths.

  • Review AI-suggested activities in the Activities module
  • Approve, modify, or override recommendations
  • Monitor student progress in real-time
4
Monitor & Intervene

Use dashboards to track progress and identify students needing support.

  • Check the Teacher Dashboard regularly
  • Review alerts for struggling students
  • Use AI recommendations while applying professional judgement
5
Formative Evaluation

Continuously assess learning and adjust pathways as needed.

  • Use built-in formative assessment tools
  • Review learning analytics
  • Adjust pathway difficulty and pacing

Frequently Asked Questions

The AI analyzes observable learning signals including response patterns, time spent on activities, number of attempts, and progression patterns. It uses reinforcement learning to identify optimal learning paths based on similar student profiles. However, all recommendations are suggestions - the final decision always rests with the teacher.

Yes, absolutely. The platform is designed with teacher agency as a core principle. You can accept, modify, or completely override any AI recommendation. The system learns from your decisions to improve future suggestions.

The platform follows GDPR guidelines and data minimization principles. Only necessary learning data is collected, and all personal information is encrypted. Students can be anonymized for analysis purposes, and data is never shared with third parties without explicit consent.

The dashboard will alert you to students who may need additional support through the "Attention Required" section. You can then review their learning history, adjust their pathway difficulty, assign remediation activities, or provide direct intervention. The AI will suggest specific strategies based on the student's profile.

Observable learning signals include:
  • Correct/incorrect responses: Overall accuracy rate
  • Number of attempts: Persistence and engagement level
  • Time spent: Engagement depth (too fast may indicate guessing, too slow may indicate difficulty)
  • Response revisions: Self-correction and metacognitive activity
  • Progression patterns: Learning trajectory over time
