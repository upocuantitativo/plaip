Initial Diagnostic Assessment

Questionnaires and diagnostic tests to identify needs, map strengths and weaknesses, and initiate the Personalised Learning Pathway.

Assessment Duration
~30 min

Competence Areas Assessed

Literacy

Reading comprehension, text interpretation, writing skills

10 questions
Numeracy

Mathematical reasoning, data interpretation, problem solving

10 questions
Digital Skills

Online safety, information evaluation, digital tools

12 questions
Citizenship

Civic knowledge, democratic participation, social responsibility

10 questions

Demographic Questionnaire

Collects basic information to contextualize learning pathways.

  • Age and education level
  • Learning preferences
  • Technology access
  • Previous digital experience

Emotional Factors Survey

Assesses motivation and self-efficacy for personalization.

  • Intrinsic motivation level
  • Self-efficacy beliefs
  • Learning anxiety indicators
  • Goal orientation

Sample Diagnostic Questions

Literacy

Read the following passage and identify the main argument presented by the author.

Tests reading comprehension and critical analysis
Numeracy

Interpret this graph showing population growth and calculate the percentage change between 2010-2020.

Tests data interpretation and mathematical reasoning
Digital

Identify which of these website characteristics indicate a reliable information source.

Tests critical evaluation of online information
Citizenship

Explain the role of the European Parliament in the EU decision-making process.

Tests civic knowledge and understanding of institutions
